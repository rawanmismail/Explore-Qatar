Special Considerations

ğŸŒ™ Ramadan

Ramadan dates vary yearly (Islamic calendar). During Ramadan:

  • Eating/drinking in public during daylight is prohibited
  • Restaurants closed during day (open for Iftar/Suhoor)
  • Reduced business hours for many establishments
  • Unique cultural experience with beautiful evening atmosphere
  • Amazing Iftar buffets at hotels
  • Respectful dress more important
